## Part 1: Statistical Analysis

### Linear Regression (Weight vs Height)
- Positive relationship identified between height and weight
- Statistically significant (p < 0.001)
- Moderate explanatory power (R² ≈ 0.21)

### Two-Sample t-test (Gender vs Height)
- Significant difference in average height between genders
- Males are taller on average

### Chi-square Test (Gender vs Activity)
- No significant association detected

## Part 2: Student Mobility Analysis

### Key Findings

- Student mobility increased significantly after COVID-19
- Average yearly student numbers rose from pre-COVID to post-COVID period
- Destination preferences showed statistically significant changes, but with very small practical impact
